What is “Okinawa Bingata”?

Okinawa Bingata, or Ryukyu Bingata is a traditional dyed craft in Okinawa. It is one of the representative dyed goods equaling to Kyo Yuzen, Kaga Yuzen, or Edo komon. In Japanese kanji, “Bingata” is “紅型.” “紅” means colors (usually, this kanji means red, though) “型” means patterns. Representative patterns of Okinawa Bingata fabric are plants or animals. Among Okinawa Bingata, deep blue dyed goods are called “Eshigata.”
